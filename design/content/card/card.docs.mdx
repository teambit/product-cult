## Component Usage

The `Card` component is a flexible container for displaying various types of content, such as product listings, article summaries, or user profiles. It supports a variety of customization options, including headers, footers, images, and different visual styles.

## Basic Card

A simple card with a title and some content.

```jsx
<Card title="Basic Card">
  <p>This is a basic card example.</p>
</Card>
```

## Card with Header, Image, and Footer

This example demonstrates a more complex card with a header, an image, and a footer.

```jsx
<Card
  title="Product Card"
  image="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1548504769-900b70ed122e?ixid=M3w3MDc2NDF8MHwxfHNlYXJjaHwyfHxhYnN0cmFjdCUyMHRlY2hub2xvZ3l8ZW58MXwyfHxvcmFuZ2V8MTc0OTU5Nzk2NXww&ixlib=rb-4.1.0"
  imageAlt="Abstract representation of data analytics"
  header={<h4>New Launch!</h4>}
  footer={<div>View Product</div>}
>
  <p>Track your SaaS metrics with beautiful dashboards and actionable insights.</p>
</Card>
```

## Interactive Card

This example shows how to make a card interactive, changing its appearance on hover.

```jsx
<Card title="Interactive Card" interactive>
  <p>This card is interactive. Hover over it to see the effect.</p>
</Card>
